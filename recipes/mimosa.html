Mimosa Eggs

Description

The simplest thing is often the best.

Take and egg, put some great mayo and voil√† !

You now have an easy and amazing appetizer

Ingredients

For 4 servings

  • 4 whole eggs
  • 1 yolk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard
  • 1/4 liter olive oil
  • Parsley

Steps

Taken from marmiton.fr

  1. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes. Then, put them in cold water to prevent overcooking ;
  2. Take the yolk, mustard and olive oil and whip them until you get your mayonnaise ;
  3. Peel the eggs, cut them and take the yolks out ;
  4. On a plate, crumble the cooked yolks and add half of it to the mayonnaise ;
  5. Put the mix inside the half-whites. Sprinkle with the remaining crumbled yolks ;
  6. Season as you wish (a lettuce bed is perfect, some olives as well).
  7. Bon appetit !
