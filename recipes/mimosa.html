Mimosa Eggs

Description

The simplest thing is often the best.

Take and egg, put some great mayo and voil√† !

You now have an easy and amazing appetizer

Ingredients

For 4 servings

  • Whole eggs - 4
  • Yolk - 1
  • Salt and pepper - to taste
  • Mustard - 1tbs
  • Olive oil - 1/4 liter
  • Parsley - to taste

Steps

Taken from marmiton.fr

Step 1
Boil the eggs for 10 minutes. Then, put them in cold water to prevent overcooking ;
Step 2
Take the yolk, mustard and olive oil and whip them until you get your mayonnaise ;
Peel the eggs, cut them and take the yolks out ;
Step 3
On a plate, crumble the cooked yolks and add half of it to the mayonnaise ;
Step 4
Put the mix inside the half-whites. Sprinkle with the remaining crumbled yolks ;
Step 5
Season as you wish (a lettuce bed is perfect, some olives as well).
Step 6
Bon appetit !
